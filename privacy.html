Privacy Policy

Overview

Athirikya builds practical digital products designed to help people navigate everyday uncertainty with clarity and confidence. This policy explains the privacy principles that apply to Athirikya products, including MyGermanFreund.

MyGermanFreund

MyGermanFreund helps users understand official German letters and everyday life in Germany. Some features may use AI-assisted processing to generate explanations, summaries, or learning content.

Document Processing

For AI-assisted letter analysis, MyGermanFreund is designed to reduce the amount of personal information sent for processing whenever possible. Supported personal information may be redacted before AI analysis.

Documents are not permanently stored by our service as part of the letter analysis process.

Information You Provide

Depending on the feature, you may provide text, uploaded document images, app usage information, or support messages. This information is used to provide the requested functionality, improve reliability, and respond to support requests.

Third-Party Services

Athirikya products may rely on trusted third-party infrastructure and AI providers to operate the service. These services are used only as needed to deliver product functionality.

Important Note

AI-generated summaries and explanations may contain errors or omissions. Always verify important dates, payments, legal references, actions, and decisions against the original document.
